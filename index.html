ABOUT US

According to the Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disability (CRPD), children with learning and behavioral challenges are part of children with disabilities. The lives of many children with disabilities are marked by deep exclusion and deprivation, compared with their peers without disabilities. This has made their functioning in school and the communities challenging. We are therefore dedicated to creating awareness and helping children with learning and behavioral difficulties as well as their families to thrive in their environment. As lawyers and social workers who have worked in the adoption field for so many years, we believe that the family is the best place for the growth and development of a child. We, therefore, ensure that children who cannot grow with their birth families for various reasons, get to grow and thrive in a family through adoption.

OUR OBJECTIVES

  • Support children with learning and behavior difficulties to achieve the best of their abilities through capacity building and service delivery.
  • Build resilience of children and families exposed to trauma, abuse, and neglect.
  • Ensure that children live permanently in families and communities that support growth and development through the adoption.
  • Build partnerships with like-minded organizations and professionals to accomplish results for children and families

OUR MISSION

To create a world where all children are supported to learn happily, be resilient, develop self-esteem, self-confidence, set goals and thrive in the right environment.

OUR VISION

To provide unmatched quality experience as the most dependable child and family welfare partner.

CORE VALUES

  • Excellence

    • We pride ourselves in being extremely good at what we do by rendering outstanding services to ensure the welfare of the child and the family.

  • Best Practices

    • We ensure that best practices are adhered to in various aspects of our services.

  • Integrity

    • We hold our integrity and that of our clients in high esteem. We therefore uphold our moral and ethical principles and that of the client.
